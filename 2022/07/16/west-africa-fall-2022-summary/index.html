WEST AFRICA 2022
Money transfer. Note that many Western Union transfers to African countries are being blocked. Online transfers may be blocked. I have had the most success with Scotiabank which offers WU transfers (I also bank here so that is convenient). If blocked, it is necessary to phone your WU resource number (Canada 1-800-235-000), have the tracking number (MTCN), confirm that you are not being defrauded, and want to continue with the transfer. It is recorded.
Suggested packing list: travel towel, sleeping bag + liner, power bank, headlamp

Visa. Required for most nationalities.
Libya has no tourists visas – only business visas. The tour company applies. Requires a LOI or hotel reservation. Once immigration approves, it notifies the pertinent home embassy. Apply only in your home country (also in Rome where Shweres has an office). Sherwes Travel +46(0)708454361 [email protected]. Ibrahim Usta
Embassy of Libya, Consular Section, Unit 1100, 170 Laurier West Avenue, Ottawa K1P5V5 (613) 842 7519, (613) 680 2009 / fax: (613) 842 8627 / [email protected]
150€ visa application fee + CAD$102.50 at the embassy (payable by debit).
Cost. 1750€ or US$ 6 nights/7 days. preferably as cash. Money transfer, use WISE: +46(0)708454361
Currency: Libyan dinar (LYD). ATMs and international credit cards don’t work. Bring everything as cash.
